<p><p>Near Field Communication (NFC) in healthcare encompasses three market types: </p><p>1. **Reader/Writer Mode** allows healthcare providers to read data from NFC-enabled devices, like patient wristbands, contributing to seamless patient identification and record management.</p><p>2. **Card Emulation Mode** enables smartphones to act as secure identification tools, facilitating contactless payments or easy access to medical records.</p><p>3. **Peer-to-Peer Mode** permits direct data exchange between devices, enhancing collaboration in treatment plans and emergency care coordination, improving overall healthcare efficiency.</p></p>
