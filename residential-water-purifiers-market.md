<p><p>The residential water purifiers market comprises primarily three types: RO (Reverse Osmosis) water purifiers, UV (Ultraviolet) water purifiers, and others. RO purifiers use a semi-permeable membrane to remove impurities and dissolved solids, making water safe for drinking. UV purifiers utilize ultraviolet light to disinfect water by killing bacteria and viruses. The "Others" category includes various filtration technologies like activated carbon filters and gravity-based systems, catering to diverse consumer needs depending on water quality and health concerns.</p></p>
