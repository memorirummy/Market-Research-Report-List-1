<p><p>The two-wheeled electric vehicle (EV) charging market comprises two main types: charging cabinets and charging piles. Charging cabinets are enclosed units that provide multiple charging points, offering a convenient solution for urban areas with space constraints, ideal for public and commercial use. In contrast, charging piles are standalone units typically installed in designated locations like parking lots or along streets, allowing individual riders to recharge their vehicles. Both serve to enhance the accessibility and convenience of charging two-wheeled EVs.</p></p>
