<p><p>Flaxseed oil is extracted using two primary methods: the squeezing method and the leaching method. The squeezing method involves mechanically pressing flaxseeds to extract oil, preserving its natural nutrients and flavor, making it ideal for culinary and health applications. The leaching method uses chemical solvents to extract oil, yielding higher quantities but potentially compromising purity and nutritional value, often used in industrial applications. Both methods cater to different market demands, balancing quality and efficiency in flaxseed oil production.</p></p>
