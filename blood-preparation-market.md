<p><p>The blood preparation market encompasses three main types: whole blood, blood components, and blood derivatives. Whole blood refers to the complete blood collected from donors, used for transfusions. Blood components are the separated elements from whole blood, such as red blood cells, plasma, and platelets, tailored for specific medical needs. Blood derivatives are products derived from blood, like clotting factors and immunoglobulins, utilized in treating various diseases and conditions. Each type plays a crucial role in healthcare and patient management.</p></p>
