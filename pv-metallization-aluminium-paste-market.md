BSF solar cells enhance efficiency by reflecting unabsorbed light back into the cell, while PERC cells improve performance through rear passivation, allowing for better electron collection. Both technologies require specialized aluminum pastes for effective metallization, catering to the growing demand for high-efficiency solar panels in the renewable energy sector.</p></p>
