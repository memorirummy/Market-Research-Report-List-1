<p><ul><li>Line Resist</li><li>Space Resist</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ArF dry resist market is segmented into line resist and space resist categories, each serving distinct applications in photolithography. Line resist is designed to create precise, narrow patterns essential for defining circuit features on semiconductors. In contrast, space resist focuses on creating clear gaps or spaces between patterns, enabling improved resolution and performance in modern electronic devices. Both types are critical for advancing semiconductor technology, contributing to higher integration and reduced feature sizes in chip manufacturing processes.</p></p>
