<p><p>Labyrinth compressors are categorized based on the number of stages they utilize for air compression. Single-stage compressors complete the compression process in one cycle, making them suitable for low-pressure applications. Two-stage compressors enhance efficiency by compressing air in two cycles, allowing for higher pressure outputs and improved cooling. Multistage compressors use three or more stages, delivering high pressure and efficiency for demanding industrial applications. Each type offers distinct advantages depending on specific operational requirements and pressure needs.</p></p>
