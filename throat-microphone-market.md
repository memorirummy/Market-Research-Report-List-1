<p><p>The throat microphone market is divided into two main types: wired and wireless throat microphones. Wired throat microphones connect directly to an audio device via cables, offering reliability and consistent sound quality, often used in military and security applications. Both types are designed to capture sound vibrations from the vocal cords while minimizing background noise.</p></p>
