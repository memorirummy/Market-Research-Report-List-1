EDTA tubes contain an anticoagulant for whole blood collection and cell counting. Plasma separation tubes are designed to separate plasma from cellular components, suitable for biochemical analyses. Other types may include tubes for specialized tests, blood culture bottles, or those with additives for enhancing sample stability and integrity across various diagnostic applications.</p></p>
