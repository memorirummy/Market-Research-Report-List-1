<p><p>The Universal Material Testing Machine (UMTM) market is categorized into two main types: metal material testing machines and non-metallic material testing machines. Metal material testing machines are designed to evaluate the mechanical properties of metals, including tensile strength, ductility, and hardness. In contrast, non-metallic material testing machines focus on testing materials like plastics, composites, and ceramics, assessing properties such as elasticity, impact resistance, and compressive strength. Both types are essential for quality control across various industries.</p></p>
