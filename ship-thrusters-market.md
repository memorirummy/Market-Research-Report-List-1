<p><p>The ship thrusters market includes various types such as azimuth thrusters and lateral thrusters, which enhance vessel maneuverability and control. Azimuth thrusters can rotate 360 degrees, allowing for precise movement and positioning, making them ideal for tugs and specialized vessels. Lateral thrusters, often installed on the hull's side, provide sideways thrust to assist in docking and mooring. Both types facilitate improved navigation capabilities, optimizing operational efficiency and safety in maritime activities.</p></p>
