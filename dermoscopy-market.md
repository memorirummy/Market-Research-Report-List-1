<p><p>The dermoscopy market is divided into two main types: traditional dermatoscopes and digital dermatoscopes. Traditional dermatoscopes are handheld devices that utilize magnification and polarized light to assist dermatologists in examining skin lesions closely. In contrast, digital dermatoscopes offer advanced features, including image capture, storage, and analysis, enabling enhanced diagnostic capabilities and teledermatology applications. Both types serve critical roles in improving the accuracy of skin diagnoses, with digital options increasingly preferred for their technological advantages and convenience.</p></p>
