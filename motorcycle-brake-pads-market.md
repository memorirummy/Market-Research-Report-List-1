<p><p>The motorcycle brake pads market primarily consists of two types: sintered and ceramic. Sintered brake pads are made from metal composite materials, offering high durability and excellent performance in extreme conditions, making them suitable for off-road and racing applications. Ceramic brake pads, on the other hand, are composed of ceramic fibers and non-ferrous materials, providing quieter operation and reduced dust. They excel in everyday riding scenarios due to their longevity and consistent braking performance, catering to a wider consumer base.</p></p>
