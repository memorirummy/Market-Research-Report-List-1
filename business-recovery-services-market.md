<p><p>Business Recovery Services encompass various market types designed to address different stages of financial distress. Administrative takeover involves appointing an administrator to manage a failing business, aiming to recover assets. Compulsory liquidation occurs when a court orders the sale of a company's assets to satisfy creditors. Creditor voluntary liquidation allows creditors to initiate winding up with agreement from shareholders. Voluntary management provides a structured plan for a company to regain stability. Other markets may include restructuring advisory and turnaround consulting services.</p></p>
