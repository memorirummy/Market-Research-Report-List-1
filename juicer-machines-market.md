<p><p>The juicer machines market is primarily categorized into three types: centrifugal, masticating, and other juicers. Centrifugal juicers use high-speed spinning blades to extract juice quickly, making them ideal for convenience. Masticating juicers, on the other hand, operate at slower speeds, crushing fruits and vegetables to retain more nutrients and enzymes, resulting in richer juice. Other juicers include hybrid models and manual options, each catering to different consumer preferences and needs, emphasizing versatility and efficiency in juice extraction.</p></p>
