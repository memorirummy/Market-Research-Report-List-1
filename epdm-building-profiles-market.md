<p><strong>The EPDM Building Profiles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sulfuration</li><li>Thermoplasticity</li></ul></p>
<p><p>EPDM (ethylene propylene diene monomer) building profiles are categorized into two main market types: sulfuration and thermoplasticity. The sulfuration market involves cross-linking EPDM through vulcanization, enhancing its durability and resistance to heat, ozone, and weathering, making it ideal for roofing and sealing applications. In contrast, the thermoplasticity market focuses on producing EPDM as a thermoplastic elastomer, allowing for easier processing and recyclability. Both types offer distinct advantages for construction and automotive uses, catering to different performance requirements.</p></p>
