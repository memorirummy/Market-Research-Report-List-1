<p><p>The hydrogel contact lens market comprises three primary types: </p><p>1. **Spherical Contact Lenses** are designed for individuals with normal vision or simple refractive errors, providing uniform correction across the lens.</p><p>2. **Toric Contact Lenses** are tailored for astigmatism, featuring varying lens powers that accommodate different orientations of the eye.</p><p>3. **Multifocal Contact Lenses** cater to presbyopia, offering multiple focal zones for clear vision at varying distances. Each type addresses specific vision requirements, enhancing comfort and visual acuity for wearers.</p></p>
