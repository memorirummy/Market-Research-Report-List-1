<p><strong>The Kraft Paper Adhesive Tape Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent Based</li><li>Rubber Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Kraft Paper Adhesive Tape market can be categorized into solvent-based and rubber-based types. Solvent-based adhesive tapes utilize a solvent to dissolve the adhesive, resulting in strong bonding and high durability, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. In contrast, rubber-based adhesive tapes use synthetic rubber as the adhesive, offering flexibility and strong adhesion, ideal for lightweight packaging and general-purpose uses. Both types are valued for their eco-friendliness and versatility in various industrial and consumer applications.</p></p>
