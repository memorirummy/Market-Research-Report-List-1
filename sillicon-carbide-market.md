<p><p>The Silicon Carbide (SiC) market encompasses various types, primarily categorized into SiC polymorphs, IV-IV SiC semiconductors, and III-V SiC semiconductors. SiC polymorphs include 3C, 4H, and 6H forms, each exhibiting distinct electrical and thermal properties suitable for different applications. IV-IV SiC semiconductors, leveraging Si and C for enhanced performance, focus on power devices. Conversely, III-V SiC semiconductors combine elements from groups III and V to provide superior electronic and optoelectronic characteristics, enriching their utility in advanced technology sectors.</p></p>
