<p><p>Armoured glass, also known as bulletproof or ballistic glass, is a specialized type of glass designed to resist penetration from bullets, explosions, or other high-impact forces. Armoured glass is widely used in high-security applications such as banks, military vehicles, government buildings, and luxury vehicles.</p><p>The Armoured Glass Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing security concerns, rising demand for advanced protective solutions, and growing investments in infrastructure development. <p><p>The Armoured Glass market includes Safe Type and Life Safety Type categories. Life Safety Type glass focuses on protecting occupants during emergencies, offering fire resistance, shatterproof properties, and smoke control, often used in hospitals, schools, and public buildings.
