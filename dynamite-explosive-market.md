<p><ul><li>Ammonium Nitrate Explosives (Powder)</li><li>ANFO</li><li>Emulsion Explosive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dynamite and explosive market includes various types such as ammonium nitrate explosives (AN), ANFO (Ammonium Nitrate Fuel Oil), and emulsion explosives. Ammonium nitrate explosives, often in powder form, are widely used for blasting due to their affordability and effectiveness. ANFO, a combination of ammonium nitrate and fuel oil, is popular in mining for its ease of handling and cost efficiency. Emulsion explosives, consisting of a water-resistant mixture, offer enhanced stability and performance in wet conditions, making them ideal for challenging environments.</p></p>
