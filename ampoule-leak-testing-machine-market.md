<p><strong>The Ampoule Leak Testing Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vacuum Decay Detection Technology</li><li>Pressure Decay Detection Technology</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ampoule leak testing machine market is categorized primarily into three types: Vacuum Decay Detection Technology, Pressure Decay Detection Technology, and Others. Vacuum Decay Detection Technology identifies leaks by measuring changes in pressure within a sealed ampoule under a vacuum. Pressure Decay Detection Technology detects leaks by monitoring pressure changes when a gas is introduced to the ampoule. The "Others" category includes alternative methods such as helium leak detection and bubble testing, offering varied options for assessing seal integrity.</p></p>
