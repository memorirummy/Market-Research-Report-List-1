<p><p>The Metal Joining Services market encompasses various techniques to bond metals effectively. Furnace Brazing involves heating components in a controlled environment to melt the filler material, ensuring strong joints. Induction Brazing utilizes electromagnetic induction to heat metals rapidly and efficiently. Electron Beam Welding employs focused electron beams in a vacuum to create precise, high-quality welds. Hydrogen Brazing uses hydrogen gas to provide a reducing atmosphere, enhancing bond strength. Other methods include laser welding and resistance welding, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
