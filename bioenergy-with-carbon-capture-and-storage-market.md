<p><strong>The Bioenergy with Carbon Capture and Storage Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oxy-combustion</li><li>Pre-combustion</li><li>Post-combustion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bioenergy with Carbon Capture and Storage (BECCS) encompasses three main market types: oxy-combustion, pre-combustion, and post-combustion. Oxy-combustion uses pure oxygen for combustion, enhancing CO2 capture efficiency. Pre-combustion involves gasifying biomass to produce a synthesis gas, where CO2 can be separated before combustion. Post-combustion captures CO2 from the flue gases after biomass has been burned. Each type offers unique advantages for reducing greenhouse gas emissions while producing renewable energy, contributing to climate change mitigation efforts.</p></p>
