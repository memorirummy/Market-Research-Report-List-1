<p><p>The Isolation Suit market comprises two primary types: Full-isolated Isolation Suits and Backless Isolation Suits. Full-isolated Isolation Suits provide comprehensive protection, covering the entire body and typically featuring sealed seams and hoods, ensuring minimal exposure to contaminants. In contrast, Backless Isolation Suits offer easier donning and doffing, making them convenient for procedures requiring rapid changes. Both suit types cater to specific healthcare needs, offering varying levels of protection based on the risk assessment of infection and contamination in medical environments.</p></p>
