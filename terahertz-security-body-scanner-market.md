<p><p>The Terahertz Security Body Scanner market is categorized into two main types: active and passive scanners. Active type scanners emit terahertz radiation to detect concealed objects by analyzing the reflected waves. They offer high sensitivity and resolution, making them effective for security screenings. Passive type scanners, on the other hand, detect naturally occurring terahertz radiation emitted by the human body, providing a non-invasive and more privacy-sensitive approach. Both types enhance security measures at airports, stations, and other high-traffic areas.</p></p>
