<p><ul><li>Water Cooled Chillers</li><li>Air Cooled Chillers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medical cooling systems are essential in maintaining optimal temperatures for medical equipment and processes. The market primarily includes water-cooled chillers and air-cooled chillers. Water-cooled chillers utilize water to dissipate heat, offering efficient cooling and are often used in larger facilities due to their space requirements. In contrast, air-cooled chillers rely on air for heat exchange, making them more suitable for smaller spaces and easy to install. Both types ensure reliable temperature management critical for patient care and equipment functionality.</p></p>
