<p><p>The protein purification market includes several techniques. Ultrafiltration uses membrane technology to separate proteins based on size. Precipitation involves adding agents to cause proteins to form solid particles for collection. Chromatography separates proteins through various interactions with a stationary phase. Electrophoresis utilizes an electric field to separate proteins by size or charge. Western blotting combines electrophoresis and membrane transfer for protein identification. Other methods include affinity purification and size-exclusion techniques, catering to diverse research and industrial applications.</p></p>
