<p><ul><li>Single-Acting Cylinders</li><li>Double-Acting Cylinders</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pneumatic cylinder market is categorized into three main types: single-acting cylinders, double-acting cylinders, and others. Single-acting cylinders use air pressure to extend and a spring to retract, making them ideal for applications where force is only needed in one direction. Double-acting cylinders utilize air pressure for both extension and retraction, providing more power and control for complex tasks. The "other" category includes specialized cylinders such as rotary actuators and compact designs, catering to niche applications.</p></p>
