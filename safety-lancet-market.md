<p><p>The Safety Lancet market is categorized into three main types based on their activation mechanisms. Push Button Safety Lancets feature a simple button mechanism for easy activation, ensuring quick and safe blood sampling. Pressure Activated Safety Lancets operate by applying pressure, triggering the lancet to pierce the skin automatically, enhancing user control and comfort. Side Button Safety Lancets are designed with a side button for activation, providing an alternative ergonomic option. Each type prioritizes user safety and ease of use in blood collection.</p></p>
