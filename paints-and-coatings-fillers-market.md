<p><p>The paints and coatings fillers market comprises several types, each catering to specific applications. High solids/radiation curing formulations feature reduced solvents, enhancing durability and environmental compliance. Powder-based coatings offer a solvent-free option with improved coverage and minimal waste. Waterborne coatings utilize water as a solvent, promoting lower VOC emissions and easier cleanup. Solvent-borne coatings, rich in organic solvents, provide excellent adhesion and durability. Other types may include specialty coatings, catering to niche markets or unique performance requirements.</p></p>
