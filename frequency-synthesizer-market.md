<p><p>The frequency synthesizer market is divided into two main types: analog and digital frequency synthesizers. Analog frequency synthesizers generate frequencies using analog components, offering simplicity and low noise, ideal for applications requiring high signal fidelity, such as radio communications. Both types serve distinct needs across industries like telecommunications, aerospace, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
