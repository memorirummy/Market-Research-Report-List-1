<p><p>The preset resistors market is categorized into single turn and multi turn types. Single turn preset resistors offer quick adjustments with a simple clockwise or counterclockwise rotation, making them suitable for applications requiring frequent, rapid changes. In contrast, multi turn preset resistors allow for finer adjustments through multiple turns, enabling precise control over resistance values. This differentiation caters to diverse applications, with single turn devices favored for convenience and multi turn devices preferred in scenarios demanding accuracy and stability in resistance tuning.</p></p>
