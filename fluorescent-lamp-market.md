<p><p>A fluorescent lamp is a gas-discharge lamp that produces light by sending an electric current through a gas, which then emits ultraviolet light. This ultraviolet light excites a phosphor coating on the inside of the lamp, resulting in visible light. Fluorescent lamps are widely used for general lighting in commercial and residential spaces due to their energy efficiency and longer lifespan compared to traditional incandescent bulbs.</p><p>The Fluorescent Lamp Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.2% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by increasing demand for energy-efficient lighting solutions, especially in emerging economies experiencing rapid urbanization. Additionally, government initiatives promoting sustainable lighting technologies contribute to market expansion. Recent trends show a shift towards integrated smart lighting systems, where fluorescent lamps are being combined with IoT technologies to enhance energy management. The retrofitting of existing lighting installations with fluorescent lamps to achieve improved energy efficiency is also gaining traction. Furthermore, innovations in lamp design and manufacturing are leading to enhanced performance and reduced environmental impact, making fluorescent lamps a popular choice amidst evolving consumer preferences.</p></p>
