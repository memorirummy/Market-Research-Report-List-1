<p><p>The liposuction equipment market encompasses various technologies designed to assist in fat removal procedures. Negative-pressure liposuction equipment utilizes suction to remove fat through cannulas, providing precise control. Ultrasonic liposuction equipment employs sound waves to liquefy fat cells before extraction, minimizing tissue damage. Power-assisted liposuction equipment features motorized cannulas that enhance the surgeon's ability to efficiently break up and remove fat, reducing physical strain. Each type offers distinct advantages for both clinicians and patients, contributing to the industry's growth.</p></p>
