<p><p>The high-pressure ball valves market is characterized by two main types: remote mounting and direct mounting. Remote mounting allows operators to position the valve away from the pipeline, providing easier access for maintenance and control in hard-to-reach areas. In contrast, direct mounting connects the valve directly to the pipeline, ensuring a compact design and minimizing space requirements. Both types cater to specific operational needs, ensuring efficient fluid control in high-pressure applications across various industries.</p></p>
