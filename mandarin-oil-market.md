<p><strong>The Mandarin Oil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absolute</li><li>Concentrates</li><li>Blends</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mandarin oil market consists of three key types: absolutes, concentrates, and blends. Absolutes are highly concentrated oils extracted through solvent extraction, offering a rich aroma ideal for perfumes. Concentrates are derived from the cold-pressing of mandarin peels, retaining essential properties but with varying potency. Blends incorporate mandarin oil with other essential oils or carrier oils, enhancing scent profiles and therapeutic benefits. These types cater to diverse consumer needs in aromatherapy, cosmetics, and flavor industries.</p></p>
