Solar-powered lights harness sunlight, making them eco-friendly and low-maintenance, perfect for outdoor use in sunny environments. Plug-in electric lights provide consistent power and are suitable for permanent installations indoors or outdoors, requiring proximity to an electrical outlet for operation. Each type caters to specific needs and settings.</p></p>
