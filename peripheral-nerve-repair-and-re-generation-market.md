<p><p>The peripheral nerve repair and regeneration market focuses on strategies to restore nerve function following injury. Key types include nerve conduits, which guide regenerating axons across gaps; nerve wraps, used to support and protect injured nerves; and nerve grafts, which involve transplanting sections of nerves to bridge gaps. Each method targets different aspects of nerve healing, with conduits promoting axonal growth, wraps offering structural support, and grafts facilitating direct nerve regeneration, thereby enhancing recovery outcomes for patients.</p></p>
