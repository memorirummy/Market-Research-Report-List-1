<p><p>The electronic knitting machine market includes two primary types: circular machines and flat knitting machines. Circular machines are designed for producing tubular fabrics, making them ideal for items like socks and seamless garments, known for high speed and efficiency. In contrast, flat knitting machines produce flat pieces of fabric, suitable for items such as sweaters and scarves, offering versatility in design and stitch patterns. Both types leverage advanced technology to enhance production capabilities and meet diverse textile industry demands.</p></p>
