<p><ul><li>Type S</li><li>Type N</li></ul></p>
<p><p>White Masonry Cement is categorized into different types, primarily Type S and Type N, based on their performance and application. Type S is a high-strength, lime-rich mix designed for use in structural applications, offering excellent bond strength and flexibility, making it suitable for reinforced masonry. Type N, on the other hand, is a general-purpose mortar ideal for non-load-bearing walls and interior applications, providing good workability and moderate strength. Both types cater to diverse construction needs, ensuring durability and aesthetics in masonry work.</p></p>
