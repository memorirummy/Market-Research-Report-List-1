<p><ul><li>Single Optical Surface</li><li>Double Optical Surface</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The accommodative intraocular lens (IOL) market is segmented into two primary types: single optical surface and double optical surface lenses. Single optical surface lenses feature a single curvature design, allowing limited accommodation and focus adjustment. In contrast, double optical surface lenses have two distinct curvature profiles, facilitating a greater range of accommodation by mimicking natural lens movement. Both types aim to enhance vision post-cataract surgery, offering various benefits in vision quality and patient satisfaction, catering to diverse vision correction needs.</p></p>
