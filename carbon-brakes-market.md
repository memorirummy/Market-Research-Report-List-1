<p><ul><li>Carbon-Ceramic Composite Materials</li><li>Carbon-Carbon Composite Materials</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The carbon brakes market consists of two primary types: carbon-ceramic composite materials and carbon-carbon composite materials. Carbon-ceramic brakes combine carbon fiber with ceramic and offer superior heat resistance and lighter weight, making them ideal for high-performance applications such as sports cars and motorsports. In contrast, carbon-carbon composite materials, made entirely from carbon fibers and resins, provide exceptional thermal stability and durability, primarily used in the aerospace industry and advanced racing applications, where extreme temperatures are common.</p></p>
