<p><p>The cable tray market comprises several types, each designed for specific installation requirements. Ladder type cable trays feature a robust design with rungs, ideal for heavy cable loads and ventilation. Tray type cable trays provide a solid surface for cable support, suitable for indoor applications. Large span cable trays enable longer distances between supports, minimizing installation points. Combined type cable trays integrate both ladder and tray designs, offering versatility for diverse cable management needs in various industrial settings.</p></p>
