<p><p>The Switchable Window market includes Electrochromic Smart Glass, SPD Smart Glass, and PDLC Smart Glass. Electrochromic glass changes tint with an electric charge, offering energy efficiency and glare control. SPD (Suspended Particle Device) glass adjusts opacity instantly, providing privacy and light control. PDLC (Polymer Dispersed Liquid Crystal) glass switches between transparent and opaque states, ideal for privacy and dynamic light management. Each type caters to applications in architecture, automotive, and consumer electronics, balancing aesthetics, functionality, and energy savings.</p></p>
