<p><ul><li>PAN Graphite Felt</li><li>Rayon Graphite Felt</li><li>Pitch Graphite Felt</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The graphite fiber felt market comprises several types, primarily categorized by the raw material used in production. PAN graphite felt, derived from polyacrylonitrile, offers excellent thermal stability and mechanical strength, making it suitable for high-performance applications. Rayon graphite felt, made from rayon fibers, is valued for its enhanced flexibility and lower thermal conductivity. Pitch graphite felt, produced from petroleum pitch, provides high thermal resistance and is often used in industrial applications requiring durability and heat insulation. Each type serves distinct applications depending on performance needs.</p></p>
