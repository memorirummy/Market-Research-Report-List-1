<p><p>5G Standalone (SA) architecture enhances mobile connectivity through various cell types catering to different environments. Femtocells serve small spaces, providing localized coverage for homes and offices. Picocells cover slightly larger areas, such as shopping malls or airports, delivering capacity and signal strength. Microcells bridge gaps in urban areas, enhancing network performance. Macrocells are large towers providing extensive coverage across cities and rural areas. Together, these cell types enable efficient deployment and scalability of 5G networks, addressing diverse user needs.</p></p>
