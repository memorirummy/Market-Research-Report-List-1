<p><p>Threadlockers are classified into three market types based on their strength levels. Low strength level threadlockers allow for easy disassembly, making them ideal for adjustable parts or frequent maintenance. Medium strength level threadlockers provide a balanced solution, offering reliable resistance to vibrations while still allowing for disassembly with hand tools. High strength level threadlockers are designed for permanent bonding, ensuring maximum resistance to harsh conditions and extreme vibrations, suitable for critical applications where components must not come loose.</p></p>
