<p><p>The industrial grippers market is primarily categorized into electric and pneumatic grippers. Electric grippers utilize electrically-driven mechanisms for precise control and adjustability, making them ideal for delicate processes and applications requiring high speed. Pneumatic grippers, on the other hand, rely on compressed air to operate, offering robust gripping force and speed, suitable for heavy-duty tasks. Each type serves distinct applications across industries, with electric grippers favored for flexibility and automation, while pneumatic grippers excel in high-speed, repetitive environments.</p></p>
