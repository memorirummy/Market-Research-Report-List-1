<p><p>The Intermediate Line Repeater market consists of three main types: Analog Relay, Digital Relay, and IP Relay. Analog Relays utilize traditional electrical signals to amplify and regenerate voice and data transmissions over long distances. Digital Relays convert signals into digital format for improved clarity and reliability, often supporting various protocols. IP Relays integrate with internet-based systems, offering greater flexibility and remote management capabilities, making them ideal for modern communication networks. Each type serves distinct operational needs in telecommunications.</p></p>
