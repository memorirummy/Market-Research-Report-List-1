<p><p>The automotive keyless entry system market is primarily categorized into two types: Passive Keyless Entry Systems (PKES) and Remote Keyless Entry Systems (RKES). PKES allows users to unlock the vehicle automatically as they approach with the key fob, enhancing convenience and security. In contrast, RKES requires the user to press a button on a remote control to unlock the doors. Both systems improve user experience and offer various security features, driving their growing adoption in modern vehicles.</p></p>
