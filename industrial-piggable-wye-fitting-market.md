<p><p>The Industrial Piggable Wye Fitting market is divided into two main types: Bypass Piggable Wye Fittings and Shutoff Piggable Wye Fittings. Bypass Piggable Wye Fittings allow for continuous flow during maintenance or inspection activities, enabling efficient pigging operations without disrupting the system. In contrast, Shutoff Piggable Wye Fittings provide a means to isolate sections of piping, facilitating repairs or modifications while preventing flow through the closed segment. Both types enhance the operational efficiency and maintenance flexibility in industrial piping systems.</p></p>
