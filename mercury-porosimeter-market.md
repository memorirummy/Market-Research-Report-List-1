<p><p>The Mercury Porosimeter market is segmented into three main types based on pressure levels: low-pressure, medium-pressure, and high-pressure. Low-pressure porosimeters are used for materials with large pore sizes, allowing for detailed analysis of porosity and permeability. Medium-pressure models cater to a wider range of materials, balancing sensitivity and speed. High-pressure porosimeters are designed for finer, denser materials, enabling precise measurement of smaller pore structures. Each type serves distinct industries, including materials science, geology, and engineering.</p></p>
