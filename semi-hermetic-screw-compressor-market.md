<p><strong>The Semi-hermetic Screw Compressor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-screw Compressors</li><li>Twin-screw Compressors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semi-hermetic screw compressor market comprises two main types: single-screw and twin-screw compressors. Single-screw compressors utilize a single rotating helical screw to compress refrigerants, often favored for their simplicity and high efficiency in low-capacity applications. Twin-screw compressors, on the other hand, use two intermeshing screws for higher efficiency and smoother operation, making them suitable for medium to high-capacity applications. Both types are widely used in refrigeration, HVAC systems, and industrial processes due to their reliability and performance.</p></p>
