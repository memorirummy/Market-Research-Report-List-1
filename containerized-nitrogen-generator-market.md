<p><strong>The Containerized Nitrogen Generator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Membrane Nitrogen Generator</li><li>PSA Nitrogen Generator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The containerized nitrogen generator market comprises two primary types: Membrane Nitrogen Generators and PSA (Pressure Swing Adsorption) Nitrogen Generators. Membrane generators utilize selective permeability to separate nitrogen from air, offering a compact and energy-efficient solution suited for smaller applications. In contrast, PSA generators use adsorbent materials to capture and release nitrogen, providing higher purity levels and better efficiency for larger operations. Both systems are adaptable, allowing for on-site nitrogen production, which enhances operational flexibility and reduces transportation costs.</p></p>
