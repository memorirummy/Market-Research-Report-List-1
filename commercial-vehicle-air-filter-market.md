<p><strong>The Commercial Vehicle Air Filter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry-type Air Filter</li><li>Wet-type Air Filter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial vehicle air filter market consists of two main types: dry-type and wet-type air filters. Dry-type air filters utilize a pleated design to trap particles while allowing airflow, making them effective for dust-laden environments. They are lightweight and require less maintenance. In contrast, wet-type air filters use an oil-soaked medium to capture contaminants, providing superior filtration, especially in humid conditions. Both types play crucial roles in maintaining engine performance, reducing emissions, and extending vehicle lifespan.</p></p>
