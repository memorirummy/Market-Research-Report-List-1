<p><p>The selective permeable membrane market comprises primarily two types: Perfluorosulfonic Acid Exchange Membrane (PFSA) and Perfluorocarboxylic Acid Exchange Membrane (PFCA). PFSA membranes are known for their high conductivity and chemical stability, making them suitable for fuel cells and electrolysis applications. In contrast, PFCA membranes offer distinct advantages in specific ion separation and are often utilized in processes requiring robust resistance to high temperatures and corrosive environments. Both types are vital in energy, environmental, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
